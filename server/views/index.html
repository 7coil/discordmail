All about Discord Mail
Get notified when you have new Mail or when your friends are online. Send instant messages. Type dmail register to activate your own Discord Mail account!

Less important commands
dmail help
Display the help menu for Discord Mail
dmail info
Get information about the bot.

DiscordMail allows you to link your Discord account to the internet!

Valid commands:

dmail register

Register for DiscordMail

dmail send email@example.com "subject" content

Send an email to the specific email, containing the subject and content specified within the command.
