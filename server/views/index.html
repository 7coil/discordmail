Unique Advantages

Professional Domain

DiscordMail can make your own Discord venture look more professional, with a specific Discord E-Mail address that's sure to impress.

Global

Because DiscordMail is just an ordinary mailbox, you can use your DiscordMail E-Mail address anywhere where you can enter your email. You can even verify your Discord Account with it... But we don't suggest it.

All in one place

DiscordMail sends your E-Mails to your DM inbox, so you can access everything all in one place.

Commands

dmail register

Register for a DiscordMail account.

dmail send email@example.com "subject" [content]

Send an email with the following subject and with the content.

dmail reply Reply-ID [content]

Reply to the email. Requires the message-id, which is found when you recieve an email in DiscordMail.
